New Tool: Action Chain

Improving and conceptualizing some essential features for Adobe's UX software.

For quite a while, users have been asking how to make one trigger affect multiple components.

Along with adjusting proximity, in the right toolbar I have adjusted the hierarchy of elements to better suit their relationships. Component is now at the top so that it appears less arbitrary and gives it precedence over the rest of the tools. The align and repeat grid tools are in between transform and layout because they are more closely associated.
